About


I am a Civil Engineering Graduate passionate about using Data Science in the field of Transportation and Construction to solve problems and create value.

I graduated with my Bachelor's in Civil Engineering from the University of Toronto. In the previous summers I worked as a summer camp counselor and volunteered as a tutor.

The most important thing I learned from studying engineering was that good engineering design is never isolated as a purely technical problem to be solved. Good designs always approach the problem 'from outside in' and considers how well they fit into our present world by beginning with how we encounter them in our daily lives.

What I'm up to now


I am currently self studying through courses on Udacity, Coursera, Udemy and others to gain essential skills in Data Analysis as a first step in my transition into a Data Science career. I am using Trello to track my progress and Toggl to track where my time is spent. In my free time, I enjoy learning language vocabulary using Anki, reading, volunteering and calisthentics.
