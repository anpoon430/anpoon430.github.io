About

I am a recent Civil Engineering Graduate passionate about Transportation Engineering and Construction Engineering.

I graduated with my Bachelor's in Civil Engineering from the University of Toronto. In my final year I pursued my interests in Public Transit Operations and Planning and Construction Engineering. In some summer months I worked as a summer camp counselor at Grace Camp and also interned at WSP.

My capstone project in my final year consisted of presenting a business case to members of CRH on alternative fuel for cement production. I also worked on a public transit group project analysing the Dufferin bus route in Toronto which is notorious for its bus bunching and delays. In my construction engineering project my group produced a overall project duration and cost estimate for an earthworks project.

The most important thing I learned from studying engineering was that good engineering design is never isolated as a purely technical problem to be solved. Good designs always approach the problem 'from outside in' and considers how well they fit into our present world by beginning with how we encounter them in our daily lives.

I enjoy learning new things in my free time which includes learning vocabulary on Anki and learning to code on Free Code Camp and online courses on Udemy.
