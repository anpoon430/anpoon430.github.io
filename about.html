About

I am a recent Civil Engineering Graduate passionate about Transportation Engineering and Construction Engineering.

I obtained my Bachelor of Applied Science in Civil Engineering from the University of Toronto. During my studies I focused on Public Transit Operations and Planning in my final year as well as Construction Engineering. In the summer months I worked as a summer camp counselor at Grace Camp in Markham, Ontario. My capstone project in my final year consisted of presenting a business case to some members of CRH Canada on alternative fuel for cement production. I also worked on a public transit group project by collecting data on the Dufferin bus route in Toronto and then analysing the data for issues and researched and proposed viable solutions. In my construction engineering project my group produced a overall project duration and cost estimate for an earthworks project.

The most important thing I learned from studying engineering was that good engineering design is never isolated as a purely technical problem to be solved. Good designs always approach the problem 'from outside in' and considers how well they fit into our present world by beginning with how we encounter them in our daily lives.

I enjoy learning new things in my free time which includes learning vocabulary on Anki and learning to code on Free Code Camp and online courses on Udemy.
